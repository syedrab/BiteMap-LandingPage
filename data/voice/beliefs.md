# BiteMap — Beliefs

What we actually think. Copy should radiate these without stating them like a manifesto.

## Core
1. **Trust comes from real people, not stars.** Anonymous 5-star ratings are noise. A creator
   who filmed the food and put their name on it is signal.
2. **Video is proof.** If there's a clip, you can judge for yourself before you go. Every spot
   on BiteMap has one.
3. **No pay-to-rank.** Placement is earned by what people watched, saved and shared — never bought.
   If we ever sound sponsored, we've failed.
4. **Local is the whole point.** "Best burger" means nothing. "Best burger in Scarborough,
   on video" means everything.
5. **The creators did the work.** We're the editors who counted the views. Credit them by name.

## How this shows up in copy
- Lead with the creator and the number, not with adjectives.
- When asked "are these sponsored?" the honest answer is always no — say it plainly.
- Treat the reader like a local who's smart and busy: give the move, prove it, get out of the way.

## What we're against
Fake reviews, list-bait SEO sludge, "10 best restaurants" pages written by someone who's never
been to the city. We're the opposite of that — and the writing should make that obvious.
