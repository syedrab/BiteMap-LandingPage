# BiteMap — Humor

Funny is the retention engine. A blog people enjoy reading sends better signals to Google
than a "complete" one nobody finishes. Be funny, then be useful.

## The style
- **Dry, self-aware narration.** "Also, dad jokes. My 14-year-old would be mortified. Anyway."
- **Deadpan superlatives.** "The drive is the price of admission."
- **Internet-native, on purpose.** Light slang, never forced. If you have to explain it, cut it.
- **Punch down at nothing.** Roast the hype, the algorithm, ourselves — never the restaurants
  or the people eating.
- **Specific > zany.** "strip mall gold, parking lot legends" beats random wackiness.

## Calibration
- Article/zine pages: dialled up. It's a zine — be loud.
- FAQ answers and schema text: dialled down. Be helpful and skimmable; one wink max.
- Legal / support / app-store copy: off.

## Test
Read it out loud. If it makes you exhale through your nose once, it's working. If it's trying
too hard for a laugh, it's louder than the food — cut it back.
