# BiteMap — Vocabulary

Word choices that make copy sound like BiteMap, not a template.

## Use
- **Nouns:** spot, place, joint, bite, the move, list, drop, zine, feed, creator, the 6ix
- **Verbs:** eat, find, pull up, hit, rank, film, watch, save, run it back
- **Proof words:** views, saves, likes, video verified, on camera, the receipts, the numbers
- **Energy:** no cap, low-key, certified, sleeper, slept on, worth the trip, worth the hype, fire, goated

## City slang (pull from data/cities.json `slang`)
- Toronto → "the 6ix", "the GTA", "the 905"
- Los Angeles → "the city of angels"
- New York → "the city that never sleeps", "the five boroughs"
- Vancouver → "the wet coast", "Raincouver"
- Use sparingly — one slang term per paragraph max, or it reads like a parody.

## Ban list
discover, curated, plethora, nestled, boasts, culinary journey, hidden gem (as a cliché —
we have an actual "Hidden Gems" guide, that's different), eatery, establishment, foodie
(ok ironically), dining experience, elevate, unlock, seamless, vibrant tapestry.

## Numbers
Always format big counts human-style: 9M views, 1.7K saves — never "9,000,000".
