Meet Alfred — Your AI Restaurant Assistant

Finding the right restaurant shouldn't require scrolling through hundreds of reviews or trusting a faceless star rating. Alfred is BiteMap's built-in AI assistant that turns a simple question into a perfect restaurant recommendation — backed by real video reviews from food creators you can actually watch.

Ask Anything About Food

Alfred understands natural language. No filters to set, no categories to browse, no keywords to guess. Just ask a question like you would ask a foodie friend who knows every restaurant in your city. Alfred processes your preferences, your current location, and BiteMap's entire library of creator video reviews to give you personalized recommendations in seconds.

Try asking Alfred things like:

  • "What's the best ramen near me?"
  • "Where should I go for a date night?"
  • "I want spicy Thai food within walking distance"
  • "Best brunch spots open right now"
  • "Where do the food creators recommend for sushi?"

Whether you are looking for a quick lunch, planning a special dinner, or exploring a new neighbourhood, Alfred understands the context behind your question and responds with restaurant picks that actually match what you are looking for. No more sifting through irrelevant results or second-guessing generic star ratings.

Recommendations Backed by Real Reviews

Most AI chatbots pull restaurant information from outdated databases or, worse, fabricate suggestions entirely. You have probably experienced this: you ask a general-purpose AI for a restaurant recommendation and it confidently names a place that closed two years ago, or one that never existed in the first place. That is not helpful — it is frustrating.

Alfred is different. Every recommendation is grounded in BiteMap's library of verified video reviews created by real food content creators. When Alfred suggests a restaurant, it is because a creator has actually been there, filmed a review, and shared their honest experience. You are not getting a guess. You are getting a recommendation backed by video evidence you can watch yourself before you decide.

Alfred doesn't hallucinate. Every restaurant Alfred recommends comes with at least one video review from a real food creator. If there is no video to back it up, Alfred does not suggest it. That is the difference between an AI that guesses and an AI that knows.

This approach solves one of the biggest problems with online restaurant discovery: trust. When you can watch a real person walk into a restaurant, order the food, and react to it on camera, you know exactly what you are getting. Alfred connects you to that proof instantly.

Learns Your Taste

The more you use BiteMap, the smarter Alfred gets about your preferences. Alfred pays attention to the patterns in how you interact with the app — the videos you watch, the restaurants you save, the cuisines you keep coming back to, and the places you skip past.

If you tend to save ramen and Korean food videos, Alfred knows you gravitate toward those flavours. If you consistently skip chain restaurants in favour of independent spots, Alfred adapts and stops recommending chains. If you always search for places with outdoor seating in the summer, Alfred picks up on that too.

This is not a one-size-fits-all recommendation engine. Alfred builds a taste profile unique to you, and that profile sharpens with every interaction. The result is that the recommendations you get in your first week on BiteMap are good, but the recommendations you get after a month are great. After a few months, Alfred understands your palate better than most of your friends do.

Your taste data stays private and is only used to improve your own experience. Alfred works for you, not advertisers.

From Question to Reservation in Seconds

The best restaurant recommendation is useless if it takes ten more steps to actually get there. Alfred is designed to collapse the entire journey from hunger to food into a single, fast conversation.

Ask Alfred → Get recommendations → Watch the video → Order or book

Ask a question. Alfred responds with tailored recommendations, each paired with a creator's video review. Watch the video to see the food, the vibe, and the experience. Then go straight to ordering delivery or booking a table — all without leaving BiteMap. The entire journey from "I'm hungry" to "food is on its way" happens in one conversation.

No more bouncing between a review app, a maps app, and a delivery app. No more opening fifteen browser tabs to compare menus. Alfred gives you everything you need in one place, and the video reviews give you the confidence to commit without overthinking it.

Whether you are sitting on your couch deciding what to order tonight or walking through a new neighbourhood looking for somewhere to eat right now, Alfred gets you from question to food as fast as possible.
