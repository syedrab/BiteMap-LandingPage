The Story

I watched thousands of food creators posting daily. Millions of views. Real impact on local restaurants.

But TikTok and Instagram weren't built for food.

No location intelligence. No direct ordering. No AI that understands YOU—your taste palette, your favorite creators' recommendations.

You watch a video. Save it. Then jump to 5 different apps to actually eat there.
Creators get views, but can't monetize the actual transaction.

So I built BiteMap: A food-first platform with AI intelligence, local focus, and direct integration—where creators actually earn from the traffic they drive.

The Problem

Inspiration Without Action 📱

Millions watch food videos on TikTok & Instagram daily, but there's no direct path from discovery to reservation or ordering. The journey ends at "save for later."

No Food Intelligence 🧠

Platforms rely on basic popularity metrics. They don't understand local food bloggers, hidden gems, or neighborhood context. No system exists to aggregate insider knowledge and surface it when you need it.

Broken Creator Economy 💸

Food creators drive massive traffic to restaurants but earn nothing unless it's a paid partnership. No affiliate model, no commissions—just free advertising.

Trust Gap in Recommendations ⚠️

Fake reviews and anonymous ratings mean nothing. You can't trust a 5-star review from someone who thinks Olive Garden is authentic Italian.

The Solution

Food Intelligence That Understands You—And Gets You There

01

Creator-Powered Intelligence

Follow trusted food voices and build your taste profile. AI learns from the creators you trust—not anonymous strangers—to surface spots that match YOUR palate.

02

From Inspiration to Action

Watch a video → Reserve a table → Order delivery. Direct integrations with UberEats, DoorDash, and OpenTable turn every scroll into a meal—no app switching required.

03

Creator Monetization Built-In

Creators earn commission on every reservation and order driven by their content. Finally, an affiliate model for food—tracking attribution and rewarding influence.

04

Local Expert Knowledge Graph

Aggregates content from TikTok, Instagram, and local food bloggers. AI understands neighborhood context, hidden gems, and authentic expertise—not just viral trends.
