Privacy Policy for BiteMap

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

BiteMap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile information (optional: profile picture, bio, full name)

1.2 Location Information

BiteMap uses your device's location services to:

  • Show you nearby restaurants and food content on the map
  • Display videos from creators near your location
  • Calculate distances to restaurants and food spots

Location Permissions:

  • We request "When In Use" location permission to provide core app functionality
  • Location data is used only while you're actively using the app
  • You can disable location services in your device settings, but this will limit app functionality

1.3 User-Generated Content

We collect content you create or upload:

  • Videos you upload
  • Captions and descriptions
  • Comments on videos
  • Reviews and ratings

1.4 Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Videos you watch, like, save, or share
  • Search queries and browsing history
  • Creators you follow
  • App interactions and preferences
  • Device information (model, operating system, app version)
  • Performance data (video load times, errors, crashes)

1.5 Analytics and Logging

We collect anonymized analytics data to improve app performance:

  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Video playback performance metrics
  • User activity patterns (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Core Functionality: Providing personalized food discovery, video feed, and map features
  • Content Delivery: Streaming videos and displaying restaurant information
  • Recommendations: Suggesting relevant content based on your location and preferences
  • Account Management: Authenticating users and managing profiles
  • Communication: Sending notifications about new content, updates, or responses
  • Analytics: Improving app performance, fixing bugs, and developing new features
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to other users:

  • Username and profile information
  • Videos you upload
  • Comments and reviews you post
  • Your likes and interactions (may be visible to others)

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Stores user accounts, videos, and app data
  • Provides authentication services
  • Location: United States
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Bunny.net (Video CDN)

  • Delivers video content for playback
  • Stores video files
  • Location: Global CDN
  • Privacy Policy: https://bunny.net/privacy

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal requests (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protection of our rights or property
  • Prevention of illegal activity or harm

3.4 Business Transfers

If BiteMap is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • API keys and sensitive credentials stored in iOS Keychain
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies protect user data
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Anonymized Analytics: May be retained indefinitely in anonymized form

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Data

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data (upon request)

6.2 Location Services

You can control location access:

  • Disable location in device settings (Settings > Privacy > Location Services)
  • Note: This will limit core app functionality

6.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of promotional notifications:

  • In-app notification settings
  • Device notification settings

6.4 Data Deletion Request

To delete your account and data:

  • Contact us at support@bitemap.fun
  • We will process deletion requests within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

BiteMap is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Users

BiteMap is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

8.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Equal service and price

Note: BiteMap does not sell your personal information.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., restaurant websites, social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
